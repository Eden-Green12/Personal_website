I am currently working with Prof. Elizabeth Eikey, trying to find unhealthy patterns in college students.
I am a strong advocate of mental health. Specifically, in the workplace. I have noticed that the workplaces may have gone from remote to on-field, but the fact that people have been home for more than 2 years now has not been taken into account. It is expected that everyone works, acts and behaves as if everything is normal. I feel that people are not mentally prepared for this change and the mental health has been affected significantly. And offices should take into account this change, and aid their employees. Many people have faced losses. And not just financial or job-wise. They have lost friends and family. In some cases, all of their family and they are the only surviving ones. Workplaces need to have systems in place to help their employees - financially and most importantly, emotionally.
